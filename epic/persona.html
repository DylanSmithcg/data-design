Steve is really creative and makes very intricate pieces, and from time to time he likes to post his art on websites and social media. He posts on sites like reddit, and on his Instagram page through his phone. Like all artists Steve will eventually encounter a drought of creativity every once and awhile and looks for inspiration and can’t work on anything until he comes up with something. He eventually feels inspired once he sees someone else’s work and learns how they made it. He likes inspiration grid for its variety of content and its easy to use look. Steve prefers the minimalistic design of the pages but thinks it could function and transition a little smoother. He enjoys when viewing a piece or series it will recommend more at the bottom but feels that it should automatically transition into another post, that way it gives a continuous scrolling feeling.
